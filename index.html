How to take the TypeRacer test

Follow these steps to complete the typing test:

  1. Select difficulty: Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard to adjust paragraph length and complexity.
  2. Start the test: Click Start. The timer will begin when you start typing (or immediately if your implementation starts on click).
  3. Type the text: Type exactly what is shown in the sample paragraph. Pay attention to punctuation, spacing and capitalization. Use Backspace to correct mistakes. Spellcheck is disabled to avoid interference.
  4. Stop or retry: Click Stop to end early. Click Retry to reset the text and timer for the current difficulty.
  5. View results: After finishing, check the Results panel for Level, Time and WPM (words per minute). WPM is calculated based on correctly typed characters/words and elapsed time.
Tips
  • Place focus inside the typing box before you begin to ensure keystrokes are captured.
  • Avoid copy/paste for valid scoring.
  • Practice with the Easy level to warm up, then increase difficulty as you improve.
